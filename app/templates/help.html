After purchasing a machine, claims are how you tell us that you need the money made from subscriptions. This means that even if you don't do any claim until the machine subscription expires, we will still give you all of your money on your next claim.

Claiming has very little actions to take.

  1. After waiting for 24 hours since you bought a machine or made a claim, click "Machines" menu available on your home page.
  2. You might need to scroll the table to your left to expose the end.
  3. Observe he last column of any of your machine and follow he instructions it gives you, it might tell you to refresh your browser. If it contains a counter, that will show how long you need to wait for the next claim.
  4. The "claim" button is exposed after the instructions are followed, and this deposits money in your balance wallet.
